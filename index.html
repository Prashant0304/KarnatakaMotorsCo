How It Works

Our Repair
Process

A transparent and systematic approach that ensures quality at every step.

Book a Service →
01

Receive & Inspect

Motor is received, logged, and thoroughly inspected. We check winding insulation resistance, bearing condition, and mechanical integrity.

02

Diagnose & Quote

Detailed fault diagnosis report is prepared. You receive a transparent cost breakdown before any work begins.

03

Strip & Rewind

Old windings are carefully stripped. New copper wire is wound to exact specifications using our precision machines.

04

Varnish & Cure

Rewound stator receives vacuum impregnation varnish treatment and is oven-cured for optimal insulation and moisture protection.

05

Test & Deliver

Full no-load and load testing performed. Motor is handed over with a test report and 90-day workmanship warranty.
